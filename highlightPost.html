The Story Behind Quiet Growth

A gentle journal about learning, building, and becoming.

Hi, I’m Chi

I’m currently studying in Finland and pursuing a second degree. My academic background started in International Business, where I developed an interest in how organizations work, how decisions are made, and how businesses grow.
Over time, my curiosity gradually shifted toward the technology side of business — especially data, digital tools, and how technology supports better decisions.
Because of that, I decided to move slightly in a new direction and start learning more about technology and analytics. I’m currently exploring areas like web development, data analysis, and IT business analysis.
Quiet Growth is where I document that learning journey — the small things I study, the tools I experiment with, and the ideas I discover along the way.
I’m not writing as an expert. I’m simply sharing what I learn, step by step.
If you enjoy calm progress and thoughtful learning, you’ll probably feel at home here.

— Chi, writing from Finland
